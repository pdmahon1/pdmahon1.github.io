Recursive Filename Renamer ("Bulk String Replace")⤤

Status: Complete (Ongoing Maintenance)

I have many video files -- we're talking thousands! -- and I was tired of manually replacing certain unicode characters with characters I wanted in their place. I made it through maybe two dozen files before realizing I was doing this terribly inefficiently. So now I have a Python script to do the work automatically. I could have used GNU's rename program, but (1) I didn't know it existed and (2) it isn't recursive anyway, which is what I needed. The README tells you more.
